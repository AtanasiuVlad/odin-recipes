Pizza Recipe

Hello everyone, this is a simple recipe for a tasteful italian pizza.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • olive oil
  • salt
  • mozzarella
  • blue cheese
  • regular cheese
  • parmesan

Steps

  1. Make the pizza dough
  2. Put the topping on it
  3. Bake the pizza for 15-20 minutes with oven heated to 200 degrees
